General Information

Amount
18-20grams for a single serving. 44-46grams for 2 servings.

Need Chart

Brew Temperature
Typical brew temperature: 202-204F

Ratio
16grams of water for every 1gram of coffee. 16:1

Grinder Adjustment
To the left is finer. Right is coarser.

Chemex

  • Great for multiple servings. Use pour over for single serving.
  • Three folds of the filter towards the spout.

Pour Over

  • Multiple or Single serving.
  • Cleaner cup than Chemex. Not as clean as the V60.

V60

  • Single Serving.
  • Cleanest cup.
Flash brew/Japanese Iced Coffee

Replace half the water with ice for a cold brew. Add flavorings and sweetner.

Moka Pot

  • Grind about 21 grams of coffee.
  • Do not use ration. Fill to mark on bottom vessel.
  • Set stove to just over medium.
  • Use 3D printed wrench to torque moka pot together.

Cold Brew

  • Use Hario container with built-in filter.
  • Grind about 122 grams of coffee.
  • Fill with water. Let sit about 24 hours at room temperature.
